## Case study

Certificate renewal is operationally critical infrastructure work: it must be done correctly, on time, and across a large enough surface area that manual execution becomes a risk. For Kafka cluster hosts, manual renewal was taking about a day and had to cover a large Linux host footprint.

I created an automated certificate renewal pipeline for Kafka cluster hosts. The work focused on turning a repetitive manual process into a repeatable automation path that could handle certificate renewal at infrastructure scale.

The pipeline reduced the operational burden on engineers and made renewal faster and more consistent. It also helped reduce the risk that routine certificate work would become urgent or error-prone because of manual steps.

The measurable result was a reduction in renewal time from 1 day to 5 minutes. The pipeline renewed 1,500+ certificates for Linux hosts, making it a concrete example of platform automation improving both speed and reliability.
